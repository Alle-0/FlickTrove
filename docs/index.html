Essential FAQ

Quick answers about FlickTrove.

Why choose FlickTrove over other tracking apps? +
Unlike traditional tracking apps that feel cluttered and ad-heavy, FlickTrove focuses on speed, privacy, and visual elegance. Built to feel ultra-smooth and native on your Android device, it features dynamic color atmospheres that adapt to movie posters, complete global cinema catalogs, and instant offline access with automatic cloud backup—all completely free and ad-free.
Is FlickTrove 100% free and offline-compatible? +
Yes! FlickTrove is a free passion project for cinema lovers. Your data is saved directly on your device for instant offline access, and automatically synchronizes securely when you are online if an account is created.
Can I migrate my watchlist from other platforms? +
Absolutely! FlickTrove features a seamless import/export tool allowing you to transfer your historical watch data, custom folders, and ratings without losing a single movie or show.
