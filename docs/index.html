Essential FAQ

Quick answers about FlickTrove.

Why choose FlickTrove over other tracking apps? +
Unlike traditional tracking apps that feel cluttered and ad-heavy, FlickTrove focuses on speed, privacy, and visual elegance. Built natively for Android with Jetpack Compose, it features dynamic poster color theming, seamless TMDB/OMDB/Trakt integration, and a true offline-first architecture with automatic cloud backup—all completely free and ad-free.
Is FlickTrove 100% free and offline-compatible? +
Yes! FlickTrove is a free passion project for cinema lovers. Your data is stored locally in a fast Room database for immediate offline access, and automatically synchronizes with Firebase cloud storage when you go online.
Can I migrate my watchlist from other platforms? +
Absolutely! FlickTrove features a seamless import/export tool allowing you to transfer your historical watch data, custom lists, and ratings without losing a single movie or show.
